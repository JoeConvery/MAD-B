The Good: Explored

In the same way that Lego is a tool for creating and relaxing, many proponents of AI art cite AI art tools as just another medium to work with. AI art also has potential uses as a form of inspiration or a starting platform of sorts. Users might have a rough idea of something they want to model, draw or paint and can feed that to an AI algorithm. From there they can quickly recieve many mock-ups of the design, seeing what works and doesn't work for them before putting pen to paper.

In creative industries like hollywood and advertising, the use of AI art as a tool to reduce costs and increase work speeds has been extremely tantalising. What's more interesting is that a recent study suggests that AI art tools could be introduced without firing any human artists.


The study by Bain & Company found that AI could be used to streamline many different types of film and tv production, reducing time and budget by as much as 25%! This would be through using AI to block out scenes, using game-engine tools and VFX programs.
